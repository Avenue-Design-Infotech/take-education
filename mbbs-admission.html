Guiding You Towards Your Dream Medical Career

Are you a recent high school graduate, uncertain about your path in the medical field? Fear not, for I am here to guide you towards your dream career.

Embarking on a journey in medicine demands dedication and passion, promising a rewarding and esteemed professional future. If you possess a strong understanding of physics, chemistry, and biology, then a medical college may be the perfect fit for you.

Entrance Exams for MBBS

Before delving into preparations, it’s crucial to determine your desired specialization within the vast realm of medical education, whether it be MBBS, BDS, Veterinary Sciences, or others.

  • NEET: The primary gateway to most medical colleges across India, except AIIMS and JIPMER. Minimum eligibility: 140 out of 720. Exam Date: May 5, 2024.
  • AIIMS: One of India’s top medical institutions. Entrance exam scheduled for May 25-26, 2024.
  • JIPMER: Conducts its own entrance exam. Exam Date: July 2, 2024.

Advantages Of Pursuing MBBS In India

  • Enrollment in MCI-approved colleges.
  • Admission through NEET, ensuring quality education.
  • Hands-on patient experience during internships.
  • No language or food-related challenges.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Minimum 50% marks in the 12th board exam.
  • Age should be between 17-25 years.
